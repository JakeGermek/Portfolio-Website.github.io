Welcome!

Welcome to my portfolio! I apprciate you checking it out. This portfolio was created to showcase some of my projects relating to SQL, Python, Tableau, and other data related processes. I wanted to learn these skills because it is something I have been interested in for a while, but didn't act on it until recently. The reason why I am trying to break into the data world is because it lines up with how I think about everything. Whether it's going to the grocery store and you're trying to get in and out as fast as possible while also trying to minimize the amount of money you spend or maybe you're working on your golf game and you want to track how many fairways you hit vs how many times you hit it in the rough (which is pretty often for me). Efficiency is important in any business as well as somebody's everyday, personal life because it can save you time, money, energy, the list could go on!

This portfolio will be a constant work in progress due to me being in a stage where I am doing a lot of self teaching through online resources, but I am excited to get to a point to showcase all that I have learned throughout my journey to becoming a data analyst!
